Research

Quintessence dynamics & EoS

Analytical & numerical studies of \(w(a)\) for pNGB potentials \(V(\phi)=\mu^4\,[1+\cos(\phi/F)]\), including late‑time oscillations and constraints from DESI DR2.

Methods: KG eq., shooting in CLASS, stiff ODE control.

Dark‑sector interactions

Parameter inference for DM–DE coupling \(\lambda\) using Monte‑Carlo pipelines; model selection via DIC/AIC with upper limits on interaction strength.

Tools: Cobaya, MultiNest, SHAP feature analysis.

Numerical inference & HPC

Scalable sampling on SLURM clusters; robust job design (MPI + OpenMP), monitoring, and plotting utilities for publication‑ready figures.

Artifacts: job scripts, plotting templates, notebooks.
